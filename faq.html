Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why should I buy pixels?

Buying pixels allows you to promote your website, brand, art, or message on a unique internet project that will remain online as part of digital history.

What kind of images can I upload?

You can upload memes, personal artwork, digital drawings, funny images, or any creative picture you want to share on the Memory Wall.

What kind of images are NOT allowed?

Images that contain nudity, violence, hate speech, copyrighted content without permission, or anything offensive are strictly prohibited. We reserve the right to remove inappropriate images.

What image formats are supported?

We accept JPG, PNG, and WEBP images. Make sure your image fits correctly inside your purchased pixel area.

What size should my image be?

Your image should match the number of pixels you purchased. For example, if you buy a 20x20 block, your image should be 20x20 pixels to display perfectly.

How many squares can I buy?

You can purchase as many pixels as you want, depending on availability. Each pixel costs $1.

Can I edit my uploaded image later?

Once your image is uploaded and the square is purchased, it cannot be changed. Make sure your image is final before uploading.

When will my image appear on the Memory Wall?

Images are displayed on the wall after purchase and approval. Moderation may take a few minutes to ensure content guidelines are met.

How long will my image stay on the wall?

Your image will remain on the Memory Wall permanently as part of the project history.

Can I link my image to my website?

Yes! When you purchase pixels you can add a link to your website so visitors can click your image and visit your page.

Is my purchase refundable?

All purchases are final. Refunds are not available once a square is bought.

Supported formats and maximum file size?

Supported formats are JPG, PNG, and WEBP with a maximum size of 70KB. Animated GIFs or higher size are not allowed to keep the wall fast and smooth for everyone.
